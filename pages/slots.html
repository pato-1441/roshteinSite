BOOK OF SHADOWS

NoLimit City

Looking for a witch themed-slot that's not too scary or too cartoonish? With its Book of Shadows slot, NoLimit City has made a great balance between the two while reinventing the classic books slots at the same time. Set in the misty dark forest, spooky, yet artistically designed, black magic symbols like an eyeball in a vial and a goat skull spin on a grid of up to 5 rows. How many rows (and hence paylines) will it have depends solely on the decision of the player. But this release hides much more, so dare you step into the shadows and start a spiritual journey?
